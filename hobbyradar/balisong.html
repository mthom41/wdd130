What is a Balisong?

Balisong is actually the name of a tool more commonly known as a butterfly knife. Cited often as appearing originally in the Philippines and occasionally cited as originating in France, the balisong is a knife with 2 handles that enclose the blade when shut and hold it straight when open. Itâ€™s a very useful tool that can be both opened and shut with 1 hand making it very convenient to work with. In modern day, balisongs are used primarily as a sort of skill or stunt toy, with the primary goal being to spin the knife around in interesting and complex ways. Mastery of momentum and finger control is a large part of this hobby.
